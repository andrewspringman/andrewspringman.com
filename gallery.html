Art & Music

Every piece here was generated with ITGrapher — a real-time Metal renderer I built from scratch on macOS. Each image is the visual record of a mathematical process: an iterated transform function applied across a coordinate space, colored by how the orbit of each point evolves. The parameters that produced each piece are embedded in the file and can never be exactly reproduced by accident.

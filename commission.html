Commission

Let's build something that matters.

I take on a small number of commissions each year — for people and organizations doing work I believe in. I'm not the right collaborator for every project, but when the fit is right, I bring everything I have.

Music

Original compositions, arrangements, and production. I've written for worship services, educational settings, film-adjacent projects, and pure concert performance. My background is jazz and classical theory, which means I can write in almost any style while understanding why it works.

Artwork

Generative and mathematical art — prints, large-format digital originals, and pieces designed for specific spaces. Every work is produced with ITGrapher, my custom Metal renderer, which means each piece is genuinely one-of-a-kind: the result of specific mathematical parameters no one has set exactly that way before.

Software

Custom tools for good causes. I've built everything from church management utilities to generative art engines to music education apps. I prefer working with nonprofits, educators, and faith communities — organizations that are trying to do something real and don't have the budget for enterprise vendors.
